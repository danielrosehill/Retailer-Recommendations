**Category:** Power Protection / UPS

Purchasing UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) units in Israel. A surprisingly niche site dedicated specifically to power protection equipment.

- While power quality in major Israeli cities is generally good, UPS protection is invaluable for desktop users
- Also sell more advanced power conditioning units for sensitive equipment
- As a desktop user, I've seen the value of having a UPS over and over again
